üì± Responsive Design Notes

Mobile Layout: Navigation collapses to vertical stack, hero images resize, product grid becomes single column, contact form stacks vertically

Tablet Layout: 2-column product grid, adjusted spacing and font sizes, contact form maintains two-column layout

Desktop Layout: Full 4-column product grid, horizontal navigation, optimal spacing, side-by-side contact info and form

üéØ Interactive Elements
  • Homepage: "Learn More" button shows welcome message
  • About Page: "Read More" toggles additional content, achievement cards highlight on click
  • Products Page: Search functionality, category filters, product modals with multiple close methods
  • Contact Page: Form validation, clear error messages, success feedback
