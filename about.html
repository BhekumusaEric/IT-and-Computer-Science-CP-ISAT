Our Story

QuickMark Spaza Shop was established in 2018 with a simple mission: to provide our community in Soweto with convenient access to essential groceries and household items at affordable prices.

What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted neighborhood shop that serves over 200 customers daily. We take pride in being more than just a shop - we're a vital part of the community fabric.

Our founder, Mrs. Sarah Mthembu, started this business to create employment opportunities in the community and ensure that fresh, quality products are always available to local families.

Today, we employ 5 local residents and continue to support community initiatives and local suppliers whenever possible.

Mrs. Sarah Mthembu, Founder & Owner
